## Overview

This API was designed around managing student and staff information in a school environment.

It gave me a strong opportunity to deepen my understanding of Go, REST-style API design, and clear technical documentation.

## Focus Areas

- Building maintainable backend services in Go
- Managing structured school data and future expansion paths
- Designing endpoints that are easy to reason about and document

## Notes

The broader vision around this work included course management, schedule generation, and other administration tools that could grow over time.
