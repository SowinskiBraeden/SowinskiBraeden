Proud Projects


One Project I am proud of is my XP-System, it helps a teacher create new assignments for classes, students can view those assignments. The student can earn XP that unlocks different levels to compare with your peers and gives a basic understanding of your progress. Students are not limited to classes, they can view any assignment for any class, so their creativity and curiosity is not limited.
